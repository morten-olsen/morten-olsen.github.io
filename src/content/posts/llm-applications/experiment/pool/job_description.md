# Mid-Level Backend Engineer

**Team:** Platform Engineering
**Location:** Remote (EU time zones) or Berlin office
**Level:** Mid (3–5 years professional experience)

## About the Role

We're a 90-person B2B SaaS company building workflow automation tools for mid-market operations teams. Our platform processes around 40 million events per day, and we're growing both the customer base and the engineering team. The Platform Engineering team owns the services that the rest of engineering builds on: our core API, the event ingestion pipeline, the job-runner that powers customer-defined workflows, and the data store layer that backs all of it.

We're hiring a mid-level backend engineer to join this team. You'll own meaningful pieces of the platform end-to-end, work closely with product and customer-facing engineers, and help shape how we scale the core systems over the next two years.

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement backend services in Python (FastAPI / async) and the supporting infrastructure on AWS.
- Own a service or subsystem end-to-end — from design discussion through deployment, monitoring, and on-call rotation.
- Improve the reliability and observability of the platform: tracing, structured logging, SLO definition, and the alerting that hangs off them.
- Work with PostgreSQL as the primary data store. Design schemas, write migrations, and reason about query performance under load.
- Contribute to the platform's event-driven backbone (currently Kafka-based) — both producer and consumer side.
- Participate in code review, technical design discussions, and incident reviews. We expect you to push back when something feels wrong.

## What We're Looking For

**Required:**

- 3+ years of professional backend engineering experience in production environments.
- Strong working knowledge of Python and at least one mainstream web framework (FastAPI, Django, or Flask).
- Comfort with PostgreSQL (or another relational database) at a level beyond CRUD — you've designed schemas, debugged slow queries, and thought about indexing.
- Hands-on experience operating services in a cloud environment (AWS preferred; GCP or Azure acceptable). Containers, CI/CD, basic infrastructure-as-code.
- A track record of shipping production code that others depend on and being responsive when that code breaks.

**Strongly preferred:**

- Experience with event-driven systems (Kafka, RabbitMQ, Kinesis, or similar) in production.
- Observability tooling experience — distributed tracing, metrics, structured logs.
- Comfort writing technical documentation and design proposals for non-immediate teammates to read.

**Nice to have:**

- Experience in a fast-growing SaaS company (post-product-market-fit, pre-enterprise-scale).
- Contributions to open-source backend tooling.
- Some exposure to one of: Go, Rust, or another statically typed backend language.

## What We Don't Care About

- Whether you have a CS degree.
- Whether you've worked at "name-brand" companies.
- How fast you can solve LeetCode puzzles.

## Compensation & Logistics

- Salary band: €75,000 – €95,000 depending on experience and location.
- Remote-first; quarterly off-sites in Berlin.
- 30 days vacation, learning budget, standard EU benefits.

Apply by sending a resume and (optional) short note about something you've built or shipped that you're proud of.
